# Moving the Mountain — AI Adapting to You

A talk about adapting AI tools to your natural thinking process, rather than the other way around.

## View the slides

- **Lightning talk (8-10 min):** [dannyockilson.github.io/ai-moving-the-mountain](https://dannyockilson.github.io/ai-moving-the-mountain/)
- **Extended talk (~25 min):** [dannyockilson.github.io/ai-moving-the-mountain/?version=extended](https://dannyockilson.github.io/ai-moving-the-mountain/?version=extended)

## Core thesis

> "Adapt the tool to you, not you to the tool."

The prevailing approach to AI adoption is: change your process to work better with AI. This talk inverts that — keep your natural thinking process (messy, analog, visual) and make AI meet you there.
